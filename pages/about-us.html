We are a non-governmental and non-profit association

The association's primary goal is the affirmation of the Italian language and culture, as well as the promotion, encouragement and advancement of Italian-Macedonian cooperation.

But it does not exclude other youth activities of a social nature, Erasmus programs, etc. Strategic partner and supporter in our work is the Embassy of the Republic of Italy in Skopje.

OUR MISSION

Quo Vadis Ad Astra is a sustainable organization that generates international collaborations, implements projects and activities for development and advancement of the skills and knowledge of young people, enriching the competences for cultural and linguistic diversity, developing moral and civic values, and strengthening the capacities of civil society.

OUR VISION

To be a leader in the region in affirming, establishing and strengthening international cooperation on a cultural and social level and a catalyst for change for a better civil society.

DESCRIPTION OF THE ACTIVITY

The Citizens' Association Quo Vadis Ad Astra was founded and has been operating since 2021. It is already a sustainable and active organization, with an established organizational structure and network of partners and collaborators.

Among its members are several experts from various fields, doctors of science, masters, qualified educators and successful project implementation teams. The organization is particularly proud of its numerous young volunteers, aged 16 to 29. It has its own office space, a training room and complete technical equipment.

During its several years of existence, it has already had a long series of successfully implemented projects, cultural exchanges, workshops, humanitarian events, etc.

Quo Vadis Ad Astra is recognized and registered as a Youth Organization by the Agency for Youth and Sports of the Republic of Macedonia.
